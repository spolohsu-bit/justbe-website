Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 28, 2025

JustBe is designed with a "privacy-first" approach. We believe that your personal growth and mindfulness journey should be private. This policy outlines how we handle your information.

1. Data Collection

JustBe does not collect, store, or transmit any of your personal data to external servers. We do not have user accounts, and we do not use tracking or analytics tools.

2. Data Storage

All your settings, custom presets, and session data are stored locally on your device. If you enable iCloud sync, your data is securely stored in your personal Apple iCloud account and is not accessible by the developers of JustBe.

3. Third-Party Services

JustBe does not integrate with third-party social media platforms or advertising networks. We do not sell or share any data because we don't collect it in the first place.

4. Children's Privacy

JustBe is safe for all ages and does not collect any information from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our website.

隱私權政策

JustBe 採用「隱私優先」的設計理念。我們相信您的正念之旅應該是私密的。本政策說明了我們如何處理您的資訊。

1. 資料收集

JustBe 不會收集、儲存或傳送 您的任何個人資料至外部伺服器。我們沒有使用者帳號系統，也不使用任何追蹤或分析工具。

2. 資料儲存

您的所有設定、自訂預設組合和練習數據都 儲存在您的裝置本地。如果您開啟了 iCloud 同步，您的資料會安全地儲存在您個人的 Apple iCloud 帳戶中，JustBe 的開發者無法存取這些資料。

3. 第三方服務

JustBe 沒有整合第三方社群媒體平台或廣告網絡。我們不會出售或分享任何資料，因為我們從一開始就沒有收集資料。
