Just Be is grounded in modern neuroscience:

  • Polyvagal Theory: We use breathwork like the "Physiological Sigh" to engage the ventral vagal system, switching off the stress response.
    üå¨Ô∏è Try it: A built-in downshift switch
    1. Inhale through the nose.
    2. Top it off with a second short inhale.
    3. Long sigh out through the mouth.
    Repeat 3 times and notice the shift.
  • Somatic Regulation: Anxiety lives in the body. Through "The Shake Out" and tactile feedback, we release stored charge from the nervous system.
  • Neuroplasticity: The brain is plastic. Daily micro-habits build new neural pathways, cultivating long-term emotional resilience.
